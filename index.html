The problem
The AI transition will impact communities, customers, and voters more profoundly than anything in our lifetimes
We are barreling toward that future without ever combining sentiment modeling, polling, grassroots engagement, and thought leadership in ways that can drive meaningful solutions. AI adoption, workforce displacement, and regulatory change are generating multi-billion dollar decisions with no real-time read on how Americans are experiencing them. The early terrain of energy costs, data centers, and privacy will resolve. Nobody is prepared for what comes next.
